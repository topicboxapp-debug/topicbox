Features

Everything you need to manage email questions

TopicBox sits quietly in your Gmail sidebar and brings order to your inbox with AI classification.

🤖

Multi-AI Classification

Choose your AI provider — Google Gemini (free), Claude by Anthropic, or OpenAI GPT. Bring your own API key, pay only for what you use.

🏷️

Smart Topic Detection

Emails are automatically classified into customizable topics like Career, Finance, Health, Family, and more. Fully configurable to match your content.

🌍

Country & Language Detection

Automatically detects the sender's country and language from email content, helping you understand your global audience.

🎲

Episode Planner

Pick random diverse questions for your podcast or show episodes. Ensures topic variety across episodes with smart diversity algorithms.

📋

Export & Scripts

Copy episode scripts to clipboard or export as HTML. Formatted and ready to use for your content production workflow.

☁️

Google Drive Sync

Episode history syncs to your Google Drive automatically. Access your data across browsers and never lose your episode history.

📊

Dashboard & Filters

Filter emails by topic, country, language, and date range. Sort by newest or oldest. See stats at a glance with the dashboard view.

📅

Date Range Classification

Classify emails from today, this week, this month, or up to the last 2 years. Control exactly which emails get processed.

🎨

Gmail-Native Design

Built with Material Design 3 to match Gmail's modern look. Feels like a native part of your Gmail experience, not an add-on.

How It Works

Up and running in 2 minutes

No complex setup. Install, sign in, and start classifying.

1

Install Extension

Add TopicBox from the Chrome Web Store. Click the extension icon to get started.

2

Sign in with Google

Connect your Gmail account. TopicBox reads your unread emails securely through Gmail API.

3

Add Your AI Key

Enter your Gemini, Claude, or OpenAI API key in settings. Gemini keys are free from Google.

4

Classify & Organize

Hit "Classify" and watch your emails get sorted by topic. Pick questions for episodes and export.
